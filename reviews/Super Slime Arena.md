# Super Slime Arena

## Summary
Super Slime Arena is a chaotic party‑fighting game with positive Steam sentiment but limited broader coverage. A GamingOnLinux review is notably negative about controls and overall feel.

## Review signals
- Steam user reviews: Mostly Positive, 76% positive (42 reviews, all‑time).
- GamingOnLinux review: Critical coverage citing controller setup issues and sluggish gameplay.

## Notes from reviews
- Steam feedback trends positive but the review count is small.
- The GamingOnLinux reviewer recommends skipping it due to control issues and lack of single‑player.
