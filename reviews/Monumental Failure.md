# Monumental Failure

## Summary
Monumental Failure is a physics‑based monument construction game with positive Steam sentiment and a single published critic review. Coverage outside Steam is sparse.

## Review signals
- Steam user reviews: Positive, 86% positive (22 reviews, all‑time).
- GameGrin review: 7/10, praising short‑burst co‑op fun while noting it can feel one‑note over time.

## Notes from reviews
- Steam feedback is generally positive but limited in volume.
- The GameGrin review frames it as best in short sessions, especially with friends.
