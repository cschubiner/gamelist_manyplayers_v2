# Obliteracers

## Summary
Obliteracers is a chaotic party racing game with mixed Steam sentiment but positive critic reception. Metacritic shows a solid metascore with a small review count.

## Review signals
- Steam user reviews: Mixed, 65% positive (167 reviews, all‑time).
- Metacritic: Metascore 78 based on 8 critic reviews; user score 7.6 based on 11 ratings.

## Notes from reviews
- Critics generally praise the fast, party‑friendly chaos, while Steam users are more divided.
